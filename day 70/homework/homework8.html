Recipe: Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 150g Ground Beef
  • 1 Onion, chopped
  • 2 Garlic cloves, minced
  • 400g Canned tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Boil the spaghetti according to the package instructions.
  2. In a pan, heat the olive oil and saut√© the onions and garlic.
  3. Add the ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Pour in the canned tomatoes and let it simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Serve the sauce over the spaghetti.
